### 3. Prepare Store Listing Assets

**You'll need these for Google Play Console:**

#### Required Assets:
- [ ] **App Icon:** 512x512 PNG (no transparency)
  - Extract from your current icon or create new
  - Location: Check `android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-*/ic_launcher.png`
  
- [ ] **Feature Graphic:** 1024x500 PNG
  - Promotional banner for Google Play
  - Create with your app branding
  
- [ ] **Screenshots:** At least 2, up to 8
  - Phone screenshots: 16:9 or 9:16 aspect ratio
  - Min 320px, max 3840px
  - Show key features (recipe browsing, cooking mode, etc.)

#### Required Text:
- [ ] **Short Description:** 80 characters max
  - Example: "Discover and cook amazing recipes with AI-powered meal planning"
  
- [ ] **Full Description:** Up to 4000 characters
  - Describe features, benefits, how to use
  - Include keywords for search

- [ ] **App Title:** 30 characters max
  - "QuickDish" (you already have this)

**Time:** 2-4 hours (depending on design skills)
